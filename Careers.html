Benefits

ASET Partners, our employees are our greatest asset. We compensate our highly skilled employees with salaries that reflect the talent they bring to our clients. We also offer a comprehensive benefit package including:

  • Competitive Salaries & Compensation Plans
  • Paid Time Off – 10 Federal Holidays plus 15 paid personal days
  • Health Care – Comprehensive health insurance, including dental insurance
  • Education Reimbursement – We pay up to $5,000 for tuition for courses relevant to an employee’s career
  • Retirement- Retirement benefits include a 401(k) savings plan
  • Flexible Spending Accounts for qualified medical, dependent care, and transportation costs
  • Employer-Paid Life Insurance, Short-term & Long-term disability
  • Profit Sharing and Bonuses – We are committed to recognizing those employees that go the extra mile through cash compensation and profit sharing
  • Employee Achievement and Merit Awards – A program to reward and recognize high achievement
  • Flexible Work Schedules – Opportunities include compressed workweeks and flextime
  • Telecommuting – Options for employees to work offsite
Supporting Our Veterans
The V3 Program

ASET Partners is a proud member of the Virginia Values Veterans (V3) Program, a program dedicated to providing transitional resources and assistance to Veterans who now call Virginia home. We here at ASET recognize the value and experience our Veterans bring to the workforce, and through the V3 Program, we are able to implement nationally recognized best practices in recruiting, hiring, and retaining these highly-skilled and dependable Veterans. TECH Internship Program

Technical Edge & Community-Support by HUBZone (TECH) Internship Program

ASET Partners is seeking bright, articulate, and self-motivated undergraduate and graduate students for Part-time, Year-round, Paid internships. Students interested in emerging technologies, cyber security as well as numerous other non-technical disciplines such as accounting, writing, graphic design, and political science are encouraged to apply. This program is available to students interested in and capable of conducting research, performing detailed analysis, applying theory and research to real world problems, writing press releases, editing papers and proposals, doing graphical design and other research and management activities.

General Program Requirements and Information
  • Applicants must be US Citizens.
  • Applicants must be students in high school, undergraduate or graduate study.
  • Applicants must provide their own computer and must have high speed internet access.
  • The program is virtual and encourages the application of students from across the United States, however, ASET will not relocate individuals as part of this program.
  • This is a part-time, paid internship program.
  • Applicants are accepted, and internships are awarded, on a continual basis throughout the year.
  • To qualify for a position, students must reside in a SBA-designated HUBZone as defined on the SBA website at https://map.sba.gov/hubzone/map/.

TECH is designed to keep ASET at the cutting edge of technology while helping develop future technology and security professionals with a focused understanding of the technologies and challenges most critical to the Federal Government. This program leverages students’ education, familiarity and understanding of the latest cutting-edge technologies, security products, digital trends and research efforts to keep ASET Partners at the forefront of technology and in turn provide ASET Partners’ clients with the best possible solutions. Outputs from the TECH program are used to maintain ASET Partners awareness of the technology horizon, drive ASET Partners strategy and approach and ensure the best services for ASET Partners clients.

This internship opportunity is open to students studying in any field at any location in the United States. Although preference is given to those in the technology or computer based-fields, the TECH program will consider students from non-technical fields including business, graphic design, English and Political Science, etc.
